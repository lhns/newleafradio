Credit where credit is due

Original Composer: Kazumi Totaka

These tracks are from Animal Crossing: New Leaf and their existance is in thanks to the work by original composer Kazumi Totaka. For more information on contributions Kazumi has played in Animal Crossing as well as various other Nintendo intellectual products click here!

Original Website by: Terrible_At_Games

The original website was made by Terrible_At_Games on newleafradio.glitch.me. Glitch suspends websites that are not updated regularly so I uploaded it to GitHub and made a few adjustments.

New Leaf Music Modified and Extended by: JezDayy

Rather than rip the loopable tracks straight from the game, their mp3's were gathered from the youtube channel Jez run by JezDayy. Seriously check out their channel. Their work involves way more than just extending Animal Crossing audio and I've personally checked out their channel in the past for music to use for studying (especially the raining tracks).

I plan to never feature their personal works here as I would hate to steal their work and rob them of any monetization that comes from watching works that they have personally compiled/composed themselves. What will be here are just the extended tracks featured from their channel.

New Horizons Music Collected from: Cevan

Tracks for the New Horizons soundtrack option were collected from the playlist created by Cevan located here. I really do suggest that you check out their channel personally. They have a lot of the original soundtrack available, even extending to the cool new tracks for all the different museum exhibits!

Rain Background Music Collected from: Sleep and Relaxation Music

To add the rain background noise for the New Horizon soundtracks, I collected and overlayed some simple rain ambience on the original audio. If you are interested in this track by itself or want to listen to more relaxing music to chill out to, you can find their channel here.

Wild World Music from: P4lf

The music for Wild World was collected from the playlist by P4lf located here.
